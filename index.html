Short Bio

I am an applied development economist with main research interests in microfinance, demography, conflict, migration, ... and side interests in sports economics.

From 2015 to 2021, I was Maître de Conférences (Associate Prof) @ Unicaen (Université de Caen, Normandie) and researcher @ TREE (TRansitions Energétiques et Environnementales, UMR CNRS 6031).
